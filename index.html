Naomi. She's the Queen of White Space.

who?

Naomi White is a fiercely independent human. A driven designer, fanatic problem solver, brand and business strategist, serial entrepreneur, speaker and confident leader.

She’s also a grafter. She gets stuck into every corner of a business; from forming financial and market strategy, to full development planning and scope. From UI design to support ease-of-use and retention, through to building out a full visual brand and reputation. She gets into the nitty gritty of the entire operation, and loves nothing more.

Naomi is Co-Founder and CEO of community-based games and entertainment company STARKADE. What once started as a passion project, has now become her primary focus, leading and working alongside an extremely talented team of nine.

She also supports her best friend with brand at Minimum Skincare. And continually feels guilty at the lack of time she has to help co-curate content for the fantastic Logo Archive (Great Britain).
